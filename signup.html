The Data-Room application is a unique financial data monitoring system

that can be integrated with any legacy systems like the SAPs, Oracle, Tally etc.

to collate, analyze and visualize data within minutes.

Massive data sets are collated into groups that are stored in big data format and

represented to stakeholders and specific industry users in uncluttered and easy to understand formats.

The system with its in built ML tools can help users make critical decisions, support available data,

identify discrepancies in the data and make smart predictions about financial health
